NOVEMBER


     It all happened so fast... It was normal until then...


    Everyone likes to have a surprise for their birthday. May it be a surprise gift, or party but never a surprise disaster. But that was what happened to me.

    The day before doesn't give a hint for a disastrous day tomorrow. It was very sunny. Very peaceful. How foolish, becuase that was the sign we were waiting.
    Remember it still makes me cry; makes me sad. That event made me reaize so many thing; everything can go away.

      Remembering this now, feels like it all happened yesterday.


     With that, we have to go back here in Cavite and start anew.
